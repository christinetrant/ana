Boxing Babes

Recreational boxing sessions are for women of all sizes, abilities and fitness levels. A full body workout. Learn efences, combinations, footwork and partner work with pads whilst getting fit. A session you get to learn the ropes of boxing, have fun whilst you exercise.

Gloves are provided but please do bring your own if you have some.

Every 2nd and 4th Saturday of each month 10am-11am The Bridge (woman only gym) 73-81 Southwark Bridge Rd, London, SE1 0NQ £10 per person (Card and Cash payments accepted)

BoomBox

Recreational boxing is for women of all sizes, abilities and fitness levels. A full body workout. Learn defences, combinations, footwork and partner work with pads whilst getting fit. At these sessions you get to learn the ropes of boxing, have fun whilst you exercise.

Please bring your own gloves if you have, limited pairs available.

Every Tuesday 7pm-8pm Lounge 55 East, 53-55 East Street, SE17 2DN £10 (Card and Cash payments accepted)
