About Ana

Mission Statement

Femaleboxingcoach wants to run and create as many women only sessions around south London to enjoy the sport, skill and benefits of boxing. Offering a safe space and sessions which empower individual woman, mentally and physically within a sport that men dominate.

My sessions are for all abilities and fitness levels, a full body workout, believe. Women can come and learn defences, skill, combinations, foot work, partner work and pad work. There is no pressure and is non- contact it's very much about focusing on fitness, helping you build confidence, making new friends and strengthening your body and mind.

My ethos is: To work from the inside out, if you feel good mentally, then you look good

I wanted to be a female only boxing coach because I can motivate individuals and especially reassuring woman that they can be great at boxing, a sport that is dominated by men.

I want to and my plan is to reach as many women as I can so they can also feel the benefits of boxing and continue to grind on in this busy society we live in.

What is your excuse? No matter your size, race or fitness ability, give it a try, what have you got to lose?

I am a passionate and dedicated level 2 England boxing coach, level 3 personal trainer. A coach who smashes barriers, Inclusion is key Deaf clients welcome qualified level 2 British Sign Language (BSL) and LGBT women welcome.

Come feel the benefits and get that endorphin rush for yourself!
