About Ana

I am a passionate and dedicated level 2 England boxing coach, level 3 personal trainer. A coach who smashes barriers, Inclusion is key Deaf clients welcome qualified level 2 British Sign Language (BSL) and LGBT women welcome.

Featured Classes

Boxing Babes

A full body workout. Learn defences, combinations, footwork and pad work. Sessions are focused on fitness and partner work, helping you build confidence, make friends, and strengthen your body and mind.

All abilities and fitness levels welcome!

Boom Box

Recreational boxing or This Fitness/Boxfit session is for women of all sizes, abilities and fitness levels. A full body workout. Learn defences, combinations, footwork and partner work with pads whilst getting fit. At these sessions you get to learn the ropes of boxing, have fun whilst you exercise.

Please bring your own gloves if you have, limited pairs available.
